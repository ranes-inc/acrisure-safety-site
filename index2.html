Powerful Features & Tools

Acrisure Safety provides all the tools you need to manage safety compliance in high-risk environments.

  • Safety Planner & Inspection Reporting

    Streamline your safety planning and inspection process with our Safety Planner. Capture site conditions through photos, document critical details, and receive AI-assisted analysis for creating targeted safety plans.

    • Analyzes worksite conditions by providing photos and details
    • Creates customized safety reports and plans
    • Enables photo-based documentation and validation
    • Tracks inspection history and provides a history of your safety plans

  • Log-It: Incident Reporting & Worksite Snapshots

    Document worksite conditions and report safety incidents in real-time with our powerful Log-It system. Capture photos, add detailed notes, and instantly communicate concerns or circumstances to your team.

    • Real-time worksite documentation with photos
    • Instant communication of site conditions
    • Detailed incident reporting & tracking
    • Investigation & corrective action management
    • Historical site condition records
    • OSHA compliance documentation

  • Training Management

    Deliver, track, and certify safety training directly from our platform. Customizable training that can be assigned with tracking and certifications.

    • On-demand and webinar formats
    • Mobile-friendly training videos and materials
    • Built-in testing and certification tracking
    • Safety toolbox talks and technical training
    • Multi-language support (English/Spanish)

  • Document Library & Management

    Access and manage documentation in one centralized, secure location. Our Document Library provides mobile-friendly access to important documents, reports, and training materials with powerful organization and control features.

    • Centralized document storage and management
    • Granular access control and permissions
    • Searchable documents and focused visibility for employees
    • Attachable to Acrisure Safety's other tools

  • Forms Builder & Management

    Create, assign, and track custom forms with our intuitive drag-and-drop builder. From safety inspections to equipment checks, manage all your form needs in one place.

    • Drag-and-drop form builder with rich components
    • Dual system: a Library for general access and Assignments
    • Support for text, images, videos, signatures, and file uploads
    • Track submission status and manage approvals
    • Perfect for safety meetings, inspections, and compliance documentation

    Common Form Types:

    • Safety Meeting Reports
    • Equipment Inspections
    • Jobsite Safety Checks
    • Vehicle Inspections
    • AI Safety Analysis
    • File Upload Forms

  • Timeclock Management

    Track and manage employee time with precision using our comprehensive timeclock system. Features location tracking, detailed entry management, and streamlined approval workflows.

    • Geolocation tracking for clock-ins/outs
    • Project cost code integration
    • Visual map view of employee locations
    • Detailed entry management and approval system

  • Messaging System

    Stay connected with your team through our centralized communication system. Send direct messages, company-wide announcements, and receive important updates through both the Employee App and Admin Portal.

    • Direct messaging between users and company-wide announcements
    • Rich text editor with formatting options
    • Push notifications for new messages
    • Organized inbox with read status tracking
